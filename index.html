FACE TURNS //

  • U - up
  • D - down
  • L - left
  • R - right
  • F - front
  • B - back
  • ' - opposite direction
  • 2 - double move

*Each side's regular direction is clockwise as if the cube was facing you.

DOUBLE FACE TURNS //

u

d

l

r

f

b

  • u - up
  • d - down
  • l - left
  • r - right
  • f - front
  • b - back

*The second layer will be one in from the indicated face. Also includes variable moves (', 2).

MIDDLE LAYER TURNS //

M

E

S

  • M - 1 layer in from the left face
  • E - 1 layer in from the bottom face
  • S - 1 layer in from the front face

*M follows the same direction as L because M is closer to L than R in the alphabet. The same idea applies for E and S.

CUBE ROTATIONS //

x

y

z

  • x - follows the R direction
  • y - follows the U direction
  • z - follows the F direction

*x follows the same direction as R because x is closer to R than L in the alphabet. The same idea applies for y and z.
