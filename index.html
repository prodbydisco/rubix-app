FACE TURNS //

  • U - up
  • D - down
  • L - left
  • R - right
  • F - front
  • B - back
  • ' - opposite direction
  • 2 - double move

*each side's regular direction is clockwise as if the cube was facing you

DOUBLE FACE TURNS //

  • u - up
  • d - down
  • l - left
  • r - right
  • f - front
  • b - back

*the second layer will be one in from the indicated face. includes variable moves (', 2) also
